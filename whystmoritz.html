Reason to visit St. Moritz

  • 1. Ski Safari for non winter visitors

    • The Engadin region's winter sports enthusiasts are spoiled for choice! You won't want your time there to end because there are so many places to visit, slopes to enjoy, and views to take in. What is the solution? Why not go on a snow safari? This ski trip will take you up 4,444 metres in altitude and over 40 kilometres of slopes, as well as the best lookout points.

    Anyone who visits the Upper Engadin for skiing or snowboarding already knows this, and newcomers should be warned: the choice of pistes is enormous, with each as appealing as the next. Many people are unaware that the snow safari allows you to combine the best of the ski resorts of St. Moritz, Corvatsch, and Corviglia in a single day.

  • 2. E-Cross Buggy Park

    • Awe-inspiring adventure in drifting snow and ice!
    Drive the world's only two-seater E-Buggies around a difficult racing track.

    You can have fun while avoiding noise and emissions thanks to the powerful e-engines. This unique and innovative offer makes use of CrossBuggies, which were developed and manufactured in Switzerland. Guests will pilot the two-seater E-Buggies around a challenging racing track. The powerful e-engines will allow drivers to have a lot of fun drifting and sliding around. The minimum driver age is 16 (with an adult passenger), and the minimum passenger age is 10 years (minimum 120 cm tall)

    Make a reservation for you and your group at the park. You can use it exclusively, and the price includes four buggies. One hour is ideal for groups of up to 20 people. Larger groups may reserve the park for up to four hours.

  • 3. Corviglia Snow Park

    • Filled with fun and action at this St. Moritz fun park!

    The "Crowland" snow park in Corviglia is ideal for freeskiers and snowboarders. There is not only a fun slope, but also an Easy Park for beginners, as well as the Blue Line or Medium Line. You can practise your freestyle skills by trying out the various kickers, rails, boxes, and other features!

    From mid-December to early April, the snow park is open (depending on snow conditions). The "Crowland" snow park in Corviglia is ideal for freeskiers and snowboarders.

  • 4. Mountain dining at Muottas Muragl

    • The historic funicular railway will take you to the top station at 2453 m above sea level, where you will be rewarded with a breathtaking view of the snow-covered, indescribably vast valley. As the last rays of the sun bathe the region in a golden glow, you will be served traditional Engadin dishes. Around 11 p.m., the last cable car will return you to the village's sea of lights.

    • A ride on the Engadin's oldest cable car to the best viewpoint in the area
    • The spectacular sunset above the Upper Engadin, followed by the brilliant starry sky and St. Moritz's sea of lights
    • A traditional Engadin dinner featuring regional specialties such as capuns (cabbage rolls) or pizokel (noodles)
