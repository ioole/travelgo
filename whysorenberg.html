Reason to visit Sorenberg

  • 1. Bikeboard

    • Summer entertainment for the young and young-at-heart. They speed down from the Rossweid to the settlement of Sörenberg on three wheels. The quick ride is the ideal way to end the Rossweid expedition.

    The "fat-scooters" have huge tyres, look great, and provide hours of enjoyment for riders of all ages. The fast-paced descent takes you from the Rossweid gondola's top station (1470 m above sea level) across beautiful mountain meadows, the sparkling reservoir, and the Schlacht alpine cheese dairy back to the Rossweid gondola's bottom station (1166 m above sea level).

  • 2. Rodelbahn Rischli

    • The summer toboggan course, which is 900 metres long and features 12 bends, meanders through verdant alpine meadows. The sledders are gently pulled up to the start by a lift. The toboggan slope is located near Hotel Rischli at the entrance to Sörenberg, straight on the major road (Hauptstrasse). Summertime fun for the whole family!

  • 3. Hinterschwarzenegg

    • The Hinterschwarzenegg piste is located just beneath the Brienz Rothorn mountain chain, and it has everything you need for a fantastic day on the slopes. As you ride one of two chair lifts, you can stretch your legs and rest. And once there, you have a beautiful perspective of the surrounding Emmental valley before descending the Hinterschwarzenegg piste to Sörenberg, which is wide all the way and not too steep - ideal for broad swings with carving skis. A piste stop at the Schwand ski lodge is also never regretted, with the historic cow shed serving up all kind of regional specialties and popular slopes meals.

    Some important information.

    • Hinterschwarzenegg is the starting point (1690 m a.s.l.)
    • Accessibility of starting point: chairlift
    • Destination: Sörenberg Platz (1166 m a.s.l.)
    • Length: 3.5 km
    • Altitude difference: 524 Meter
    • Level of difficulty: easy (normal slope)

  • 4. Brienzer Rothorn

    • Views from this mountain of mountains are first-rate all-around, with breathtaking panoramas. Take in the views of the iconic Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau peaks, as well as 690 other peaks. Add in the glistening waters of Lake Brienz, and you've got a dream come true! You will be spoiled in the summit restaurant Rothorn, which has a beautiful panoramic terrace. With luck, you will also catch a sight of the enormous ibex colony and be able to capture a memento photograph.

    The cable car from Sörenberg to this wonderful location takes only ten minutes in both summer and winter.

  • 5. Gipfelrestaurant Rothorn (Summit Restaurant), 2350m

      • Culinary Delights

    • Candlelight dinner on the Brienzer Rothorn (Apéritif, Chinese Fondue, and dessert).

      • Specialties

    • Views of the Bernese Alps, including the Eiger, Mönch, and Jungfrau, as well as Lake Brienz and, of course, Sörenberg. The Brienzer Rothorn serves as the starting point for the Nesslewäng Free Ride region. Throughout the winter, all pedestrians can ride up and down the Rothorn lift for CHF 10.

      • Other Information

    • Height: 2350 m a.s.l.
    • Local place name: Rothorn
    • Accessibility: Mountain Train/Ski Lift/Ski Run
