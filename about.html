About us

TravelGo was founded in 2022. We offer 24/7 customer assistance for locals and foreigners, as well as 40 distinct local payment options, both online and offline. TravelGo is a major participant in the lifestyle sector, with reservations for varieties of local sites and activities, as well as gourmet directories. TravelGo also offers finance, payment, and insurance options for the underbanked through our Financial Services products to help consumers manage their different financial pain points in their Travel & Lifestyle journey.

    Objectives :


  • Providing access for users to discover and purchase lifestyle activities
  • Portfolio that includes transport booking services such as flight tickets as well as access to the largest accommodation inventory in Southeast Asia
  • Provide information on travelling

TravelGo is here to enrich your daily lives with the integrated offerings in a single platform that span travel, local services, and financial services. TravelGo began as an online flight and have since expanded to give a variety of packages (and counting), allowing millions of customers to have seamless access to their travel, local services, and financial needs, enriching their experiences.

TravelGo is quickly becoming Southeast Asia's most popular travel and lifestyle booking app. The road is far from ended now that TravelGo is poised to expand and help everyone around the world achieve their lifestyle goals through ongoing innovation.
