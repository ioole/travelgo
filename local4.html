Langkawi

The exterior of Yellow Cafe is all yellow, very conspicuous! It is a barbecue restaurant facing the sea, whether it is barbecued meat or seafood, it is very delicious! In addition to delicious food, it is also the liveliest bar in Jinnah Beach at night! The environment here is very good, the outdoor seats can see the sea.
The Rice Field Museum is an ecological place adjacent to the sea, but full of an idyllic and rural atmosphere. Here you can learn about rice cultivation, see and even touch on the traditional techniques and tools used in rice field planting and harvesting.
Oriental Village is a cluster of businesses that provide food and beverages, as well as souvenirs and activities and experience-based attractions. Located at the foot of Majingzhangshan Cambrian Geological Forest Park on the west coast of Langkawi, Oriental Village is home to Langkawi SkyCab and Langkawi SkyBridge, a famous tourist attraction in Malaysia
Langkawi Underwater World is one of the most-visited aquariums in Southeast Asia. You can see more than 5,000 species of fish and more than 500 species of underwater life through a 15-meter-long tunnel. The fish feeding time here is 03:00 pm every day. The underwater world also has a 4D screening theater to satisfy tourists' social enjoyment~ There are two kinds of penguins, Rockhopper and Blackfoot, and seals here!
This Upside-Down Art Museum focuses on recycling, and they turn all the waste into magic! You can see the refrigerators, furniture, beds, cabinets, toilets, and even cars used in the upside-down house, all of which are physical objects, very special! Everything is backwards! You must think beyond the box in order to let your imagination run wild! Pallets, oil barrels, scrap wood, and unwanted furniture and decorative objects are used to make the majority of furniture.
Eagle Square, Dataran Lang, is a gigantic sculpture of an eagle preparing to take flight that is one of Langkawi's most well-known manufactured attractions. The 12 meter-tall statue is one of the island's most readily recognisable landmarks, greeting guests arriving by ferry. Dataran Lang, on the southeast part of Pulau Langkawi, boasts exquisite small fountains, terraces, and bridges, as well as beautiful vistas of Kuah Bay, making it the ideal location for those postcard-perfect holiday images When you come to Langkawi, of course, you have to check-in and take pictures here! The huge eagle statue is the coordinates of Langkawi.
